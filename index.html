Project Title

CDR based Customer care and Convergent Billing

Customer

Bharat Sanchar Nigam Ltd., (BSNL)

Description

The Objective of the project is to transform BSNL’s legacy meter based systems to advanced CDR (Call Detailed Records) based systems, there by giving them an edge over private players. The CDR project comprises of Integration & Customization of around 30 different products which will be used for various day to day operations of BSNL. The project will be implemented across India with four Data centres in 300+ SSAs (Secondary Switching Area).

The main highlights of the project are :

To integrate various CSC (Customer Service Centres) across the zones there by providing better service to the customers.

To provide single bill for the customer for all the services which he enjoys across the zone.

To integrate switch with OSS (Operation Support Systems) so that services can be barred & unbarred automatically without any manual intervention.

To provide easy access to CDR of customers across the zones so that disputes can be resolved quickly ensuring customer satisfaction.
